Company Discipline Portal

Manage access requests, employee records, discipline tracking, and appeals in one centralized portal.

Portal Overview

This portal is designed for employees, managers, admins, and owners to handle access, accountability, and discipline workflows.

Employee Access Requests
Employees can request access using their Employee ID and department information.
Discipline Tracking
Managers and higher can issue, review, and later edit write-ups as the system grows.
Appeal Workflow
Employees can appeal write-ups and management can review those appeals inside the portal.
Administrative Controls
Owners and admins can manage employees, signups, approvals, and maintenance controls.
